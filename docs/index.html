dash.fail

Tips via the app only save Doordash money.
Tips do not increase driver pay.

Doordash substitutes tips for the driver delivery pay.
Tips only save Doordash money, and do not increase the driver's pay.

Drivers get paid a minimum of $4 per delivery. Here is what happens to the tips:
Customer tips $0, Doordash pays the driver $5, driver gets $5
Customer tips $2, Doordash pays the driver $3, driver gets $5
Customer tips $4, Doordash pays the driver $1, driver gets $5
Customer tips $10, Doordash pays the driver $1, driver gets $10


Doordash tricks innocent users into believing their tip is going to the driver.
This is a real prompt in the checkout menu of the app, shown by tapping the "i" icon.
Using this example, a customer would believe that their $3 is being sent to the driver as a tip. While the tip may technically be going to the driver, it's also saving Doordash $3, so in the end, the driver would get paid the same if the customer tipped $3 or $0.
